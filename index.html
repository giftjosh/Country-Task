India is a country in South Asia and the seventh largest country by area

  • Capital - New Delhi
  • Currency - Rupee
  • Population - 1.4 billion
  • Main Spoken Language - Hindi, Tamil

India has a rich culture due to its variety of people living there. The temperature is mostly humid with the monsoon being the only time it rains a lot.

Due to the country's geographical posittion, its rugged relief and its distribution between the mainland and the sea, there is great variation in India's climate throughout the country.
