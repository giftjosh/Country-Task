Poland, country of Central Europe. Poland is located at a geographic crossroads that links the forested lands of northwestern Europe to the sea lanes of the Atlantic Ocean and the fertile plains of the Eurasian frontier

Poland has a moderate climate with both maritime and continental. You can count on many sunny days and rainy days and dont be surprised when the summerturns out to be quite hot or rainy. Winters are usually cold, with temperatures well below freezing.
