Greece is a country in southeastern Europe with thousands of islands throughout the Aegen and Ionian seas

  • Capital - Athens
  • Currency - Euro
  • Population - 10.72 million
  • Main Spoken Language - Greek

Greece has a Mediterranean climate with plenty of sunshine,mild temperatures and a limited amount of rainfall.

Due to the country's geographical posittion, its rugged relief and its distribution between the mainland and the sea, there is great variation in Greece's climate.
